Essential Work Travel Guide
What to Know

Pack for Function, Not Fear

Work travel is stressful enough without worrying about what you forgot. This guide helps you show up prepared, comfortable, and ready to do your job well.

Take what works. Leave the rest.

🎯 Your Packing Philosophy

When deciding what to pack, prioritize items that:

  • Prevent stress if something goes wrong
  • Let you work from anywhere
  • Help you look and feel put together with minimal effort

Your goal: Show up ready to do your job well. Everything else is optional.

💬 Reminders

  1. Check the weather in your destination.
  2. Give yourself time. Don't cut layovers or timelines tighter than needed.
  3. Comfort matters, but dress assuming your boss is on your flight.
  4. Plan for the worst case to ensure the best outcome.
✉ You're not packing for vacation. You're packing to perform. Comfort = confidence.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Clothing: Choose wrinkle-resistant, mix-and-match pieces. Wear your bulkiest shoes on travel days.
  • Tech: Assume outlets will be inconvenient. Pack adapters and a portable charger in your carry-on.
  • Wellness: Bring personal care items with you to workshops or the office. Assume you won't go back to the hotel until after dinner.
  • Travel docs: Never check your essentials. Keep ID, meds, chargers, and one outfit in your carry-on.

✎ My Travel Notes

Jot down anything specific to this trip: dietary needs, key contacts, outfit plans, etc.
