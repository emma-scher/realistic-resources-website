Why I'm Doing This

The real reason I'm building free AI tools—and why it matters.

I'm Emma, a data science consultant who builds AI solutions for companies.

And here's what I've learned: businesses optimize for what makes them money. That's not good or bad—it's just how it works.

But it means the innovation happens where the profit is. And your daily problems? Organizing paperwork, managing schedules, simplifying complicated processes—those aren't where the money is.

So the technology exists. The capability is there. It's just not being prioritized for real people solving real problems.

I could wrap those problems in a pretty AI package and sell them back to you for $50 a month. That's the standard playbook.

But that's not why I'm here.

I'm here to build these tools. For real.

A Bit About Me

I spent a decade as a professional chef, managing complexity in real time, building systems under pressure, and making things work with whatever was available. When chronic illness made that career physically unsustainable, I pivoted into data science and AI consulting.

I live with multiple invisible disabilities, including EDS, POTS, and severe food allergies. These conditions mean intentional planning and contingency systems aren't optional for me—they're how I function. That lived experience shapes everything I create.

What I Focus On

  • Making AI and technology approachable for people who aren't technical
  • Tools that reduce confusion and decision fatigue
  • Navigating work and daily life with disability, realistically
  • Applying systems thinking from kitchens, consulting, and lived experience to everyday problems

Background

  • 10+ years in high-pressure, systems-driven environments
  • Former professional chef turned AI and data science consultant
  • Experience designing tools for people, not just organizations
