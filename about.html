About

The story behind Realistic Resources — and why practical beats theoretical every time.

Hi, I'm Emma

I'm an AI consultant and former professional chef with over 10 years of experience working in high-pressure, systems-driven environments. I spent a decade in professional kitchens, where success depended on preparation, adaptability, and managing complexity in real time.

Due to chronic illness and physical limitations, I transitioned out of kitchen work and taught myself data science and AI as a way to build a physically accessible and sustainable career. I now work as a technical AI consultant, designing practical, real-world AI systems for businesses — not theoretical or hype-driven solutions.

I live with multiple invisible disabilities, including EDS, POTS, and severe food allergies. These conditions create a high "energy tax" in daily life and work, requiring intentional planning, logistics, and contingency systems. My lived experience strongly informs how I think about accessibility, productivity, and system design.

I created Realistic Resources because I was tired of fluff. Everything here has been tested under real-world constraints — not ideal conditions. I don't share something unless I've used it myself and believe it can genuinely help you.

What I Focus On

  • Demystifying AI and technology for non-technical audiences
  • Creating practical tools, prompts, and frameworks that reduce confusion and decision fatigue
  • Navigating the workplace with disability in a realistic, non-performative way
  • Applying systems thinking from kitchens, consulting, and lived experience to everyday problems

My Approach

  • 10+ years in high-pressure, systems-driven environments
  • Self-taught data science and AI practitioner
  • Technical AI consulting for real-world business applications
  • Resources prioritizing clarity, accessibility, and ease of use
